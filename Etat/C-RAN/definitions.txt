	Telecommunication is the transmission of signs, signals, messages, words, writings, images and sounds or information of any nature by wire, radio, optical or other electromagnetic systems	

	Radio access network (RAN) is part of a mobile telecommunication system. It implements a radio access technology. Conceptually, it resides between a device such as a mobile phone, a computer, or any remotely controlled machine and provides connection with its core network (CN)

	C-RAN is a centralized, cloud computing-based architecture for radio access networks that supports 2G, 3G, 4G and future wireless communication standards.

	bande de base désigne une technique de transmission dans laquelle le signal est envoyé directement sur le canal après Codage en ligne sans passer par un codage canal (sans modulation).

	LTE (Long Term Evolution) est une évolution des normes de téléphonie mobile GSM/EDGE, CDMA2000, TD-SCDMA et UMTS.

	A base transceiver station (BTS) is a piece of equipment that facilitates wireless communication between user equipment (UE) and a network

	A remote radio head (RRH), also called a remote radio unit (RRU) in wireless networks, is a remote radio transceiver that connects to an operator radio control panel via electrical or wireless interface.The equipment is used to extend the coverage of a BTS/NodeB/eNodeB in challenging environments such as rural areas or tunnels.
